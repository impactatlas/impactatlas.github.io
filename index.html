What is Impact Atlas?

Designed for frontline organizations, Impact Atlas is a cloud-based technology platform that runs on desktops and Android smartphones. The platform enables entire global networks of implementers, investors and researchers to transform data at every level into actionable information to fuel powerful decision-making and improve performance.

Why use Impact Atlas?

Impact Atlas measures the impact of your program

Customize Impact Atlas according to your set of interventions. It tracks data on key indicators and allows for easy setup of complex goals and success criteria for beneficiaries. Field staff can collect geo-coded data from participants, even without a signal, and provide regional and head office staff with accurate data. Interactive dashboards provide meaningful insights to track beneficiary-level indicators across multiple program milestones.

Impact Atlas helps teams thrive in the field and office

From the head office to the field, Impact Atlas can be customized to meet unique organizational needs. It tracks staff performance, creates workflows and offers actionable insights for each role and team within your organization--including field staff, field managers and project managers. Every level will experience efficiency gains and reduce costs. With Impact Atlas, celebrate success over time and make smart improvements wherever necessary.
